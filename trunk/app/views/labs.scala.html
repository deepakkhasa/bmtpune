Anjali Diagnostics are run by Dr. Ketki Vijay Ramanan Kelkar.

Ketki completed her MBBS and MD (Pathology) from B.J. Medical College and Sassoon General Hospital. She is an Associate Consultant at K.E.M. Hospital, Pune and the founding director and head, Anjali Diagnostic Lab. Her special areas of interest include coagulation with special coagulation tests like Factor assays and Bethesda assays for haemophilia patients, thrombophilia workup for those with DVT, recurrent pregnancy. She has done over more than 1500 bone marrow procedures at various hospitals. She has been part of two international trials for haemophilic patients. At GenePath Dx, Ketki heads the pathology division and provides clinical perspective on cases presented.
